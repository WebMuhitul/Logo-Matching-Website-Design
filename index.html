Refinement plan & delivery

  1. Choose one concept.
  2. Up to 2 rounds of revisions (typography, color, spacing)
  3. Final export: AI (editable), SVG (clean), PNGs (transparent), and a small style note PDF listing hex colors and safe-area.

Safe-area, spacing & color spec

For each final logo deliverable we recommend a safe clear area equal to the height of the central icon (or 1x the capital letter height). In practice: use at least 16px (1x) of empty space on all sides for small uses and 32px (2x) for hero signage.

Primary hex values used in mockup:

  • Deep green: #2F6F44
  • Leaf green: #9CCB8F
  • Warm accent: #F3C677
  • Muted text: #6B6B6B
  • Background: #F7F8F6
